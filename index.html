How booking rides with Super works

1. Tell Super where you’re going

Describe your destination, timing, and preferences in plain language.

2. Super checks real apps

The agent operates ride apps in a browser to compare ETAs and prices. Consumer Reports has shown prices can vary for the same ride, so comparison matters (Consumer Reports).

3. You confirm the booking

Super prepares the booking flow, but you approve before anything is finalized.

4. Reuse your setup

Frequent routes, pickup notes, and app flows are saved in a computer-use cache, making repeat trips faster and cheaper.
